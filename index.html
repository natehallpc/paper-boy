TwoShields | Native American Dance & Flutist
Friday, April 4th | 7-9 pm
Park City Performing Arts
Tickets are required to attended

Enjoy an unforgettable evening of Native American dance and music, led by Nino Reyos, a proud member of the Northern Ute and Laguna Pueblo Indian Nations. As an accomplished flutist and Northern Traditional Dancer, Nino brings the spirit of his ancestors to life through mesmerizing performances featuring a stunning array of traditional dances —each showcased in vibrant regalia. Set beneath the stars, this immersive cultural experience is enhanced by Nino’s soulful flute music, filling the air with its hauntingly beautiful and meditative melodies.

Park City Gallery Stroll
Friday, April 25 | 6 -9 pm
Park City Main Street | Free to Attend

The Park City Gallery Association hosts the Last Friday Gallery Stroll, a lively monthly event celebrating artists and special exhibits across town. Held on the last Friday of each month, nineteen galleries welcome visitors and locals to enjoy light refreshments while exploring the latest art installations. Showcasing diverse collections from local, national, and international artists, this event is a perfect way to experience Park City’s thriving art scene while taking in the charm of Historic Main Street and the beauty of alpine evenings.
