2023 PC PowderFest (formerly Pink Park City)
March 11, 2023

You can ski and snowboard all winter long, but only one day a year can you hit the slopes surrounded by an amazing, cancer-fighting community and the inspiring atmosphere that is Park City PowderFest. This day on the mountain is dedicated to conquering more than just the slopes and having fun, but also conquering cancer by supporting the mission of Huntsman Cancer Institute (HCI).

Participants are encouraged to wear over-the-top costumes in conjunction with cancer they support. You could wear a yellow banana suit in support of children’s cancers, toss on a pink boa in support of breast cancer, or even bring out your inner wild side and dress up in zebra stripes to support neuroendocrine and rare cancers – the possibilities are endless! Not a skier or snowboarder? No problem! You can après at the base, grab a yummy bite, and join us starting at 3 pm for a special Spring Grüv concert featuring Element 47.
