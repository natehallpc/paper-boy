Park City Dine About
Entire Month of October
Times & Locations Vary
This culinary celebration invites food lovers to experience the vibrant and diverse dining scene of Park City. It offers an incredible opportunity to savor the culinary excellence of the town's award-winning restaurants at unbeatable prices. Whether you're drawn to upscale dining or charming cafes, there's something to delight every palate. It's the perfect occasion to explore new flavors or rediscover old favorites, all while enjoying the picturesque fall ambiance of Park City.

Participating restaurants will be selecting their own dates for involvement (check the PCRA website for updates).
Park City Wine Festival
October 3rd - 5th
Times & Locations Vary
Calling all wine enthusiasts and adventurers! The Park City Wine Festival, presented by the Canyons Village Management Association, invites you to explore a world of exceptional wines, with over 100 wineries from all over world. Indulge in expertly curated wine-pairing dinners and lunches throughout the weekend, embark on scenic wine hikes, and deepen your appreciation and understanding of wine through engaging seminars led by renowned winemakers and sommeliers.
Annual Scarecrow Festival
October 5th | 2 pm - 4 pm
McPolin Farm
The annual Scarecrow Festival is returning to McPolin Farm, bringing a fun-filled, family-friendly celebration of the season. Guests can enjoy face and pumpkin painting, savor autumn treats, and create their very own scarecrow to be showcased along the farm's picturesque trail.
8th Annual Park City Shot Ski
October 12th | 2 pm
Park City Main Street
Park City Sunrise Rotary Club and High West Distillery invite whiskey enthusiasts to raise a glass for a good cause at this lively fall tradition.

Last year, a world record was set with a 2,950-foot-long shot ski that served 1,363 shots and raising over $40,000 for the Rotary's grant program. This year, their goal is even bigger1,385 shots and an ambitious $60,000 fundraising target!
Jans Winter Welcome
October 26th | 6 pm - Midnight
Stein Eriksen Lodge
Join the Youth Sports Alliance (YSA) as they come together to celebrate athletes of the past, present, and future at the JANS Winter Welcome. This event supports young athletes and students in their pursuit of dreams and passion for sport. Proceeds from JANS Winter Welcome directly benefit students in YSA's afterschool programs and athletes training with their seven partner teams.
Howl-O-Ween on Historic Main Street
October 31st | 3 pm - 6 pm
Park City Main Street
Join in the celebration of this unique and unforgettable community event! Little ghosts and goblins are invited to enjoy Trick-or-Treating, courtesy of participating merchants. The real highlight, however, is the parade of costumed creaturesdogs in fantastic outfits and kids dressed in their Halloween best. With gourmet treats, playful tricks, and endless fun, its the perfect family-friendly celebration. Bring the kids with their "big" trick-or-treat bags, dress up your pups, and make memories that will last a lifetime. There's no Halloween experience like this anywhere else in Utah!

Please note dogs MUST be on leash.
