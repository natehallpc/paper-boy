Park City Trail Series is a 3-race series that runs from June – August. It starts with a 5K and works up to a half marathon. The courses are great for new trail runners but bring the excitement that seasoned trail runners love. You can mix and match which races you would like to sign up for or get all three at an amazing price!

LAST FRIDAY ART GALLERY STROLL
Friday, July 25th | 6:00pm - 9:00pm
Park City Historic Main Street

The Last Friday Gallery Stroll, hosted by the Park City Gallery Association, is a free monthly event where 19 local galleries open their doors, inviting visitors to enjoy light refreshments and explore new exhibits from local, national, and international artists. It’s a great way to experience Park City's vibrant art scene while strolling along scenic Historic Main Street.
